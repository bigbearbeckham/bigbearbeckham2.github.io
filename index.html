Subnautica

The underwater world is waiting!

Subnautica is an underwater survival game where you will try and survive. Keep in mind that not everything is friendly like the common stalker most likely the first hostile thing you will meet.

Subnautica is about a ship called the aurora that gets shot down by a mysterious power. Well later in the game you see a island not just an island, but the only one in the game and it has a gaint gun on. Fear not though it only shoots a few hundred feet in the air. Thats proof that it can not shoot the massive space ship.
